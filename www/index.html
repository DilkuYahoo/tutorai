How it works

Our streamlined process is designed to provide you with a tailored investment experience, making it easy to get financial advice that aligns with your unique goals. Here’s how it works in four simple steps

Step 1: Answer the Risk Assessment Questionnaire

To begin, complete our quick and easy risk assessment. This questionnaire allows us to understand your risk tolerance, financial goals, and investment experience. Your responses help our AI model tailor advice that aligns with your comfort level in market fluctuations.

Step 2: Choose Your Investment Preferences

Next, select your preferred investment options. Whether you’re interested in growth, income, specific sectors, or sustainability, our platform lets you define your preferences so your portfolio is aligned with what matters most to you.

Step 3: Receive Your AI-Generated Portfolio

Once we have your risk profile and investment preferences, our AI model goes to work. By analyzing thousands of shares and financial data, it generates a share portfolio specifically suited to your circumstances and goals. Updated daily with the latest market insights, our AI ensures that your portfolio recommendations are relevant and informed.

Step 4: Connect with a Financial Adviser (Optional)

If you’d like further guidance, one of our licensed financial advisers can assist. They’ll help you interpret your portfolio, answer any questions, and offer support as you move forward with your investment journey.
