How It Works

Simple Steps to Financial Freedom

AdviceGenie makes it easy to get personalised financial advice in just a few steps.

Step 1: Answer a Few Simple Questions

Provide basic details about your financial goals, current situation, and future plans through our user-friendly questionnaire.

Step 2: Let AI Do the Heavy Lifting

Your responses are securely processed and fed into our advanced AI model, designed to analyse your unique needs.

Step 3: Receive Tailored Recommendations

AdviceGenie's AI generates personalised financial advice, tailored to your goals and preferences.

Step 4: Expert Review & Delivery

Our Qualified Financial Advisers review the AI-generated recommendations to ensure they align with your best interests. The final, polished Statement of Advice (SoA) is then sent directly to your email.
